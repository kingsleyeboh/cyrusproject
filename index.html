About Us

The Cyrus Project is a learning platform that teaches web and software development skills to individuals who want to join the rapidly growing tech industry. Our aim is to provide practical need-to-know knowledge and personalized support to help learners achieve their goals. We focus on empowering individuals with the necessary skillset needed to take advantage of the high demand for skilled professionals in the tech industry.

The program covers the latest technologies and industry trends in web development and software development. (HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Python). The course material is designed to provide hands-on experience that will help learners to develop a strong portfolio of projects and demonstrate their abilities to potential employers.

Join the LIVE training today, and start your journey towards a rewarding career in tech. As experienced instructors we are dedicated to providing personalized support and ensuring that learners receive the guidance they need to succeed.
